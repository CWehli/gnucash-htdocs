<b>Convert GnuCash Data Files to QIF</b>

If you have to convert all your GnuCash data to QIF, then try the
<a href="http://gnucashtoqif.sourceforge.net/">GnuCash to QIF</a>
Java Applet.  It handles all GnuCash file formats from 1.6.0 onwards
(excluding business features).  
<a href="http://gnucashtoqif.sourceforge.net/">http://gnucashtoqif.sourceforge.net/</a>
